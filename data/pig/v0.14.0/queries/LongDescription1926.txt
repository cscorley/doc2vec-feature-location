Currently, Limit, Sample only takes a constant. It would be better we can use a scalar in the place of constant. Eg:



a = load 'a.txt';
b = group a all;
c = foreach b generate COUNT(a) as sum;
d = order a by $0;
e = limit d c.sum/100;
